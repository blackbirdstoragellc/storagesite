Hidden Fees

Returned Box Discount
$5/box

If you reduce waste by returning our boxes so we can reuse them, we will credit your account with a $5 discount per box.

Overweight Box Fee
$25

In order to keep our movers safe we must limit the weight of boxes to no more than 50lbs. If your box weighs more than that a $25 fee will be applied.

Moving Service Fee
$70

Normal pickup and delivery is curbside. If you want us to come up to your room and move your items we're happy to do that but a $70 fee will be applied.

Non-conformist Fee
$90

If you need to schedule a pickup or delivery date other than the scheduled date for your school a $90 fee will be applied.
